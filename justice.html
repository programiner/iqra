Who is Iqra?
Iqra Firdous
12–13 years old Arai village, Poonch, J&K

Iqra Firdous was a young girl from Arai village in Poonch district, Jammu & Kashmir. She was reported missing on 30 May 2026 and her body was recovered from a river near Sathra on 3 June 2026.

The incident triggered widespread protests across the region, with residents demanding a transparent investigation and accountability. A case has been registered and police continue to investigate.

Her family and community deserve truth, transparency, and justice. Until then, we will not be silent.
